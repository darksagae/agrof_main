## Usage Instructions
**Rate of use:** 4L/ha
**Mixing:** 200 ml/20ltrs of water
**Application:** Apply as a pre emergence herbicide
**Timing:** Apply when weeds are actively growing
**Coverage:** Ensure thorough coverage of target weeds
