# Calcigro

## Supplier
Agritab Uganda Limited

## Overview
CalciGrow is a dust-free ultrafine granulated calcium mineral fertilizer that is designed to rapidly neutralize acidic soils. By doing so, it maximizes nutrient availability, optimizes NPK fertilizer and micronutrient uptake.

## Usage Instructions
- Follow recommended application rates
- Apply at appropriate growth stages

- Keep away from direct sunlight
- Ensure proper ventilation
- Keep away from children and pets
